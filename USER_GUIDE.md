## Getting Started

SlugConnect is a social networking platform exclusively for UCSC students. To use SlugConnect, you must have a valid UCSC email address (@ucsc.edu).

## Creating an Account

1. **Navigate to the Sign Up page**
   - Click on "Sign Up" from the home page or navigate to `/signup`

2. **Enter your information**
   - Email: Use your UCSC email address (must end with @ucsc.edu)
   - Password: Create a secure password

3. **Verify your email**
   - After signing up, check your email inbox for a confirmation email
   - Click the confirmation link to verify your account
   - If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or use the "Resend Confirmation Email" option

4. **Complete onboarding**
   - After email verification, you'll be redirected to the onboarding page
   - Fill out your initial profile information (name, major, college, year, interests)

## Setting Up Your Profile

Your profile is your identity on SlugConnect. A complete profile helps other students find you and connect with you.

### Profile Information

- **Name**: Your display name (can be your first name, nickname, or full name)
- **Major**: Your academic major or field of study
- **College**: Your UCSC residential college (e.g., Crown, Merrill, Porter, etc.)
- **Year**: Your academic year (Freshman, Sophomore, Junior, Senior, Graduate)
- **Interests**: Add tags or keywords that describe your interests, hobbies, or activities

### Editing Your Profile

1. Navigate to your profile page or the settings page
2. Click "Edit Profile" or the edit icon
3. Update any information you'd like to change
4. Save your changes

## Discovering Other Students

### Browse Profiles

1. Navigate to the **Discover** page
2. Browse through student profiles
3. Click on any profile card to view more details

### Search Functionality

- Use the search bar to find students by name
- Search results will show matching profiles

### Filtering Options

Use the filter sidebar to narrow down your search:

- **Major**: Filter by academic major
- **College**: Filter by residential college
- **Year**: Filter by academic year
- **Interests**: Filter by shared interests

## Connection Requests

### Sending a Connection Request

1. Navigate to a student's profile
2. Click the **"Send Connection Request"** button
3. The student will receive a notification about your request

### Viewing Incoming Requests

1. Navigate to the **Connections** page
2. View all pending connection requests
3. For each request, you can see:
   - The sender's name and profile picture
   - Their basic information (major, college, year)
   - Shared interests (if any)

### Accepting or Rejecting Requests

1. Go to the **Connections** page
2. For each pending request:
   - Click **"Accept"** to connect with the student
   - Click **"Reject"** to decline the request

### Managing Your Connections

- View all your accepted connections on the Connections page
- Connections are mutual - both users can see each other's profiles

## Managing Your Profile

### Updating Information

1. Go to **Settings** or your **Profile** page
2. Edit any field you want to update
3. Save your changes

### Privacy

- Your profile information is visible to all UCSC students on SlugConnect
- Only verified UCSC email addresses can access the platform
- You can update your information at any time

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Sign Up

- **Issue**: "Please use your UCSC email address"
  - **Solution**: Make sure you're using an email that ends with @ucsc.edu

- **Issue**: Email confirmation not received
  - **Solution**: Check your spam folder, or use the "Resend Confirmation Email" option

### Can't Log In

- **Issue**: "Invalid login credentials"
  - **Solution**: Double-check your email and password. Make sure your email is verified.

- **Issue**: Forgot password
  - **Solution**: Use the password reset feature (if available) or contact support

### Profile Not Saving

- **Issue**: Changes not persisting
  - **Solution**: Make sure you're logged in and click "Save" after making changes. Refresh the page to verify changes were saved.

### Connection Requests Not Working

- **Issue**: Can't send connection requests
  - **Solution**: Make sure you're logged in and have completed your profile. You cannot send requests to yourself.

### Search/Filter Not Working

- **Issue**: No results showing
  - **Solution**: Try clearing filters or using broader search terms. Make sure there are other users with matching criteria.

## Tips for Best Experience

1. **Complete Your Profile**: A detailed profile helps others find and connect with you
2. **Add Interests**: The more interests you add, the easier it is for others with similar interests to find you
3. **Be Active**: Regularly check for new connection requests and update your profile as needed
4. **Be Respectful**: Remember that you're connecting with fellow UCSC students - be kind and respectful
